## 8) Qualification questions (copy/paste)

1. What breaks if nothing changes in the next 30 days?
2. Which decisions are currently blocked?
3. Where are records fragmented or untrusted?
4. Who owns final approval internally?
5. Can your team provide required access quickly?

Decision rule:

- `4-5 yes`: proceed
- `3 yes`: proceed with strict scope
- `0-2 yes`: defer
