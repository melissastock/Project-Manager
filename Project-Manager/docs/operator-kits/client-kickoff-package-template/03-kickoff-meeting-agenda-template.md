# Client Kickoff Meeting Agenda (60 minutes)

Use this agenda in your first live kickoff call after SOW signature and payment confirmation.

---

## Meeting Setup

- Client:
- Engagement:
- Date/time:
- Attendees:
- Operator lead:
- Decision owner:

---

## 0:00-0:10 — Outcomes and Scope Confirmation

1. Confirm the engagement objective in one sentence.
2. Confirm scope in and scope out.
3. Confirm success criteria and acceptance standard.

Script:

"Today we are confirming exactly what we are delivering, how we communicate, and how we make decisions so this engagement moves quickly without confusion."

---

## 0:10-0:20 — Delivery Method and Timeline

1. Review stage-gated workflow:
   - intake complete
   - sprint execution
   - package and readout
   - acceptance and close
2. Review timeline and milestone dates.
3. Confirm any hard deadlines and constraints.

---

## 0:20-0:30 — Roles, Ownership, and Escalation

1. Confirm single decision owner.
2. Confirm project-side owners for:
   - business decisions
   - data access
   - legal/compliance signoff
3. Confirm escalation path and response SLA.

---

## 0:30-0:40 — Communication and Update Model

1. Confirm channels (email/portal/meeting).
2. Confirm weekly update day/time.
3. Confirm decision-response SLA.
4. Confirm how scope changes are handled (change order only).

---

## 0:40-0:50 — Data and Security Controls

1. Confirm sensitivity categories.
2. Confirm secure upload and access controls.
3. Confirm no sensitive material via unmanaged channels.
4. Confirm auditability and approval checkpoints.

---

## 0:50-1:00 — Immediate Next Steps (First 7 Days)

1. Confirm first intake artifacts due from client.
2. Confirm first deliverable due date.
3. Confirm next checkpoint meeting date.
4. Confirm kickoff completion and "work start" moment.

---

## Kickoff Close Statement

"We now have confirmed scope, owners, communication, security controls, and first milestones. Work starts immediately under this structure."

---

## Post-Meeting Checklist

- [ ] Kickoff notes sent within 24 hours.
- [ ] First tasks assigned with owner and due date.
- [ ] First update date and agenda sent.
- [ ] Open risks and dependencies logged.
- [ ] Client confirmation received on notes.
